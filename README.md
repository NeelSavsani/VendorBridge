# VendorBridge - Procurement & Vendor Management ERP

## Overview

VendorBridge is a Procurement & Vendor Management ERP designed to streamline and digitize procurement operations for organizations.

The system provides a centralized platform for managing vendors, RFQs (Request for Quotations), quotations, approvals, purchase orders, invoices, and procurement analytics.

This project was developed as part of the Odoo Hackathon.

---

## Problem Statement

Organizations often face procurement challenges due to manual processes, fragmented vendor communication, delayed approvals, and lack of visibility into purchasing activities.

VendorBridge solves these challenges by providing:

- Vendor Management
- RFQ Management
- Quotation Comparison
- Approval Workflows
- Purchase Order Generation
- Invoice Generation
- Procurement Tracking
- Analytics & Reporting

## System Workflow

1. Procurement Officer creates an RFQ.
2. Vendors receive RFQ invitations.
3. Vendors submit quotations.
4. Procurement team compares quotations.
5. Approval workflow is initiated.
6. Approved quotations generate Purchase Orders.
7. Invoice is generated automatically.
8. Invoice can be printed or emailed.
9. Activities are recorded in logs and analytics.
